# Content Model

## Article

Articles are for news, campaign updates, policy notes, and local housing stories.

Fields:

- `title`
- `slug`
- `publishedAt`
- `summary`
- `mainImage`
- `body`

## Site Settings

Global settings for the public site.

Fields:

- `title`
- `description`
- `ctaLabel`
- `ctaUrl`
